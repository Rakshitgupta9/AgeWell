WhatsApp Guide

Learn how to use WhatsApp effectively - from basic messaging to advanced features.

Getting Started with WhatsApp

1. Installing WhatsApp

1

Open your smartphone's app store (Google Play Store for Android or App Store for iPhone)

2

Search for "WhatsApp"

3

Tap "Install" or "Get"

4

Wait for the installation to complete

Watch: How to Install WhatsApp

2. Setting Up Your Account

1

Open WhatsApp after installation

2

Enter your phone number when prompted

3

Verify your number using the code sent via SMS

4

Add your name and profile picture (optional)

Watch: Setting Up Your WhatsApp Account

Basic Messaging

Sending Messages

1

Tap the chat icon (message bubble) in the bottom right

2

Select a contact from your list

3

Type your message in the text box at the bottom

4

Tap the send button (paper plane icon)

Watch: How to Send Messages on WhatsApp

Sending Media

1

Open a chat

2

Tap the paperclip icon

3

Choose what you want to send:

  • Document
  • Camera
  • Gallery
  • Audio
  • Location

Watch: How to Send Media on WhatsApp

Voice and Video Calls

Making a Voice Call

1

Open the chat with the person you want to call

2

Tap the phone icon at the top

3

Wait for the person to answer

Watch: How to Make Voice Calls on WhatsApp

Making a Video Call

1

Open the chat with the person you want to call

2

Tap the video camera icon at the top

3

Wait for the person to answer

Watch: How to Make Video Calls on WhatsApp

Screen Sharing in Video Calls

1

Start a video call

2

Tap the screen share icon during the call

3

Select what you want to share

Watch: How to Share Screen in WhatsApp Video Calls

Groups

Creating a Group

1

Tap the three dots menu in the top right

2

Select "New group"

3

Select contacts to add to the group

4

Add a group name and photo

Watch: How to Create WhatsApp Groups
