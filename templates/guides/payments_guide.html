Learn about safe online payment methods, digital banking, and security best practices.

Understanding UPI

What is UPI?

1

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a real-time payment system that allows instant money transfers between bank accounts

2

Popular UPI apps include:

  • Google Pay
  • PhonePe
  • Paytm
  • BHIM

Watch: Understanding UPI

Setting Up UPI

1

Download your preferred UPI app from the app store

2

Register using your mobile number

3

Link your bank account

4

Create a UPI PIN

Watch: How to Set Up UPI

Net Banking

Getting Started with Net Banking

1

Visit your bank's official website

2

Click on "Net Banking" or "Internet Banking"

3

Register for net banking if you haven't already

4

Set up your login credentials and security questions

Watch: How to Get Started with Net Banking

Digital Wallets

Popular Digital Wallets

1

Paytm

  • Download the Paytm app
  • Create an account
  • Add money to your wallet

2

Amazon Pay

  • Access through Amazon app
  • Link your bank account
  • Add money to wallet

Watch: Understanding Digital Wallets

Security Tips

Safe Online Payment Practices

1

Never share your OTP, PIN, or password with anyone

2

Always use secure websites (look for 'https://')

3

Enable two-factor authentication when available

4

Regularly check your transaction history

5

Keep your apps and devices updated
